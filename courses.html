Your Learning Roadmap

Stage 1 · Beginner
Statistics Fundamentals

Descriptive Stats Probability Distributions Central Tendency
2️⃣
Stage 2 · Beginner–Intermediate
Inferential Statistics
Learn to make conclusions from data using hypothesis testing, confidence intervals and ANOVA.
Hypothesis Testing Confidence Intervals t-test ANOVA Chi-Square
3️⃣
Stage 3 · Intermediate
Python or R for Statistics
Apply your stats knowledge programmatically using Python (pandas, scipy) or R (tidyverse).

4️⃣

Random Forests XGBoost K-Means Neural Networks Feature Engineering
